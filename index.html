Tired of Craigslist?
Welcome to Hodgepodge Garage! We are a technology company founded in Los Angeles, California 2021. Our focus is to bring light and attention to hard to find garage sales. We strive to help those in need by bringing extra business to their residences in hopes of making a couple extra bucks! Oh, and we didn't forget about you thrifty shoppers, looking for your own pot of GOLD! We're working diligently to help this market by: Placing your Garage Sale on the map. Relieving clutter when you're moving to a new place. Helping thrifty shoppers find items for great prices. Running your own personal business out of your very home. All the while creating the fun of an every day treasure hunt! We hope you enjoy our services and ideas, and we will work tirelessly to improve the technology and overall user experience. Now please, feel free to go and find your next little treasure!
